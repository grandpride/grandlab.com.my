Our Brand

GrandLab is built on four connected pillars: our customers, our people, our products and our facility. Every car that comes through our doors in Petaling Jaya is treated as if it were our own — and we work to build relationships that last well beyond a single visit.

Next Workshop

Our Customers

Our customers sit at the centre of everything we do. From the first WhatsApp enquiry to the moment the keys go back, we want the experience to feel considered and unhurried. We explain what a car actually needs rather than what is easiest to sell, and we would rather talk someone out of a service than take on work that will not benefit their vehicle.

Our People

Detailing is a craft, and craft comes down to the people holding the machine polisher. Our team is trained in-house on coating preparation, paint correction, film application and interior work, and no one works unsupervised on a customer car until their finish is consistent. The same faces are here year after year, which is why the standard stays where it is.

Our Products

We only work with products we are willing to warranty. That means the full Gyeon Q² ceramic range and Koch Chemie for coating, ONE+ for paint protection film, and NAKA and KTN for wrapping. Each is chosen for how it performs in Malaysian heat, humidity and rain rather than for what is cheapest to stock, and every coating we apply is backed by our own GrandLab e-Warranty.

Our Facility

Coating and film work need a clean, controlled space, so our PJS 13 facility is built around that — dedicated detailing bays, a separate coating and PPF area, and a full mechanical workshop under the same roof. While your car is with us, the GrandPride Lounge is a comfortable place to wait, work or simply have a coffee. See the workshop for yourself.
