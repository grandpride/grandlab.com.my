Ceramic Coating

A lasting, liquid shield

A ceramic coating chemically bonds to your paint, forming a durable, glass-like layer that protects against UV rays, chemical stains, and the everyday grime of the road — while delivering an unmatched depth of gloss.

We apply the full Gyeon Q² range — from Cancoat to the flagship Mohs and Infinite — as well as Koch Chemie ceramic protection. Every coating is applied in a controlled environment after thorough decontamination and paint preparation, and is backed by our own GrandLab e-Warranty.

Paint Protection Film

Invisible armour

Paint protection film is a transparent, self-healing layer applied over your paint to absorb the impact of stone chips, scratches and road debris — keeping the surface beneath in factory condition.

Our ONE+ PPF is precision-cut to your vehicle and applied by hand, from single high-impact panels to full-body coverage. Available in gloss and matte finishes, it guards the areas most exposed to daily wear without altering the look of your car.

Wrapping

Reinvent your look

A colour wrap transforms the entire character of your car — and protects the original paint underneath, preserving its value for resale.

Using NAKA and KTN wrapping films, we offer a broad spectrum of colours and finishes, from gloss to satin and matte. Each wrap is precision-applied panel by panel for a seamless, factory-like result that turns heads.
